Signal variation alert settings

SmartOLT compares optical signal levels between scans and detects significant changes, both drops and increases.

These settings define when a PON should be marked as Warning, Unstable, or Critical.

Warning: At least one ONU on the PON had a signal change equal to or greater than the Signal variation threshold.
Unstable: Enough ONUs crossed the Signal variation threshold, or the largest change on the PON exceeds the Large signal variation threshold.
Critical: An ONU is at or below the critical signal level configured on the OLT, or repeated signal variations were detected within the time window.

Any signal change larger than this value will be marked as Warning.
